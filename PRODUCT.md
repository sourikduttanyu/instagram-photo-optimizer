# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Anyone who posts to Instagram — content creators, photographers, casual posters, social media managers. They arrive mid-workflow: photos ready, needing to compress before posting. Fast-paced, zero tolerance for friction. They care about output quality and don't want to think about settings.

## Product Purpose

Client-side batch image compressor built specifically for Instagram. Resizes to 1080px, binary-searches maximum JPEG quality under a target file size, warns on unsafe aspect ratios, downloads as ZIP. All processing happens in the browser — no upload, no server, no account.

Success: user drops photos, gets optimized files in under 30 seconds, zero quality anxiety.

## Brand Personality

Fast, warm, precise. Gets out of the way. Feels like a reliable tool made by someone who actually posts on Instagram — not a SaaS product or a generic converter. Warm without being cutesy. Precise without being cold.

## Anti-references

None stated.

## Design Principles

1. **Speed is the product** — every interaction should feel instant or show clear progress. No waiting in the dark.
2. **Warm, not clinical** — tool UIs can have personality. This one is approachable and human, not sterile.
3. **Trust through transparency** — show exactly what happened: original size, compressed size, quality used, dimensions. No black box.
4. **Zero setup** — sensible defaults, optional controls. New users should never need to touch settings.
5. **Precision over decoration** — every visual element earns its place by communicating information or guiding action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigable (drop zone, buttons, download triggers). Contrast ratios ≥ 4.5:1 for body text, ≥ 3:1 for large/UI text. Status states (processing, done, error) not color-only — include text labels. Reduced-motion safe (no layout animations).
